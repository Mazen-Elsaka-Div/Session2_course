🎤 What is INPUT?
INPUT
📥
Input = Sending information TO the computer
  • ➡️YOU give information TO the computer
  • ➡️You tell the computer what to do
  • ✅The computer LISTENS and receives it
💡 Think of it like talking to a friend! When YOU speak — that is INPUT. You are giving information to someone.
⌨️ Input Device 1 — The Keyboard
  • ⌨️Types letters A, B, C…
  • ⌨️Types numbers 1, 2, 3…
  • ⌨️Writes words and sentences
  • ⌨️Has special keys: Enter, Space, Delete
INPUT ✓ — It sends typing TO the computer!
🎯 Find the letter M on your keyboard right now!
🖱️ Input Device 2 — The Mouse
  • 🖱️Single click — selects items
  • 🖱️Double click — opens files & programs
  • 🖱️Right click — shows extra options
  • 🖱️Scroll wheel — moves up and down
INPUT ✓ — It sends clicks & movement TO the computer!
🎯 Show me: left click, then right click!
🎤 Input Device 3 — The Microphone
  • 🎤Records your voice to the computer
  • 🎤Used in video calls (like Zoom!)
  • 🎤Used to give voice commands
  • 🎤Used to make recordings & podcasts
INPUT ✓ — It sends your voice TO the computer!
🎯 Right now you are using a microphone!
📷 Input Device 4 — The Camera / Webcam
  • 📷Takes photos and sends them to computer
  • 📷Records videos for the computer
  • 📷Used in video calls to show your face
  • 📷Used to scan documents and QR codes
INPUT ✓ — It sends pictures & video TO the computer!
🎯 Your webcam is on right now — wave hello! 👋

🏠 Wait — There Are MANY More Input Devices!
You use INPUT devices every day at home — even without a computer! 🎮📱
TV Remote 📺
Press buttons to tell the TV what to show — INPUT!
Game Controller 🎮
Buttons send your moves to the console — INPUT!
Touch Screen 📱
Your finger tap sends info to the tablet — INPUT!
Voice Command 🗣️
"Hey Siri / Alexa!" — your voice is INPUT to a smart device!
💡 Any time YOU send information to a machine — that is INPUT. The machine listens to you!
🔊 What is OUTPUT?
OUTPUT
📤
Output = Receiving information FROM the computer
  • ⬅️The computer gives information TO YOU
  • ⬅️The computer shows or plays its answer
  • ✅YOU see, hear, or read the result
💡 Think of it like a friend replying to you! When THEY speak back — that is OUTPUT. The computer is giving you information.
🖥️ Output Device 1 — The Monitor
  • 🖥️Shows pictures and photos
  • 🖥️Plays videos and movies
  • 🖥️Displays games on screen
  • 🖥️Shows text, words and numbers
OUTPUT ✓ — It shows information FROM the computer!
🤔 What are YOU looking at right now?
🔊 Output Device 2 — Speakers
  • 🔊Plays music and songs
  • 🔊Plays sounds in games
  • 🔊Lets you hear people talk in video calls
  • 🔊Reads out text from the computer
OUTPUT ✓ — It plays sounds FROM the computer!
🎵 You can hear my voice right now — that is a speaker!
🖨️ Output Device 3 — The Printer
  • 🖨️Prints documents on paper
  • 🖨️Prints homework and school work
  • 🖨️Prints photos and pictures
  • 🖨️Turns computer information into paper!
OUTPUT ✓ — It brings information OUT of the computer!
🤔 Have you ever used a printer at home or school?
🎯 All OUTPUT Devices — Can You Name Them?
OUTPUT = Receiving information FROM the computer
Monitor
We SEE pictures and text
Speakers
We HEAR sound and music
Printer
We READ pages on paper
👉 Point at each one — can you say what it does?
🏠 Wait — There Are MANY More Output Devices!
The computer talks back to you in lots of ways — even at home! 📺🎧
Headphones 🎧
Sound comes OUT of the device — you hear music or calls. That's OUTPUT!
Television 📺
Your TV shows pictures & plays sound — it is a big OUTPUT device!
Smart Speaker 🔔
Alexa / Google Home plays music & talks back — that's OUTPUT!
💡 Any time a machine gives information BACK to you — with sound, light or a picture — that is OUTPUT!
The Simple Rule 💡
📥
INPUT
WE tell the computer something
Keyboard · Mouse · Mic · Camera
⟷
📤
OUTPUT
The computer tells US something
Monitor · Speakers · Printer
Easy way to remember: INPUT goes IN  —  OUTPUT comes OUT! 🎯
