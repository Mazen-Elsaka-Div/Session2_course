🔧 What is HARDWARE?
HARDWARE
🔧
Hardware = The physical parts of a computer that you can TOUCH
  • ✋You CAN touch it, hold it, and pick it up
  • 🏗️It is made of metal, plastic, and wires
  • 👀You can SEE it on your desk
  • 💪The word "HARD" is inside "Hardware" — it is solid!
💡 Think of your body! Your arms, legs, and eyes are like HARDWARE — they are the physical parts that do the work.
🖥️ Hardware Example 1 — The Monitor
  • 🖥️The screen that shows pictures and text
  • 🖥️You can touch the screen with your hand
  • 🖥️It sits on your desk — it is a real object
  • 🖥️Made of glass, plastic, and electronic parts
HARDWARE ✓ — You CAN touch it!
🤔 Look at your screen right now — that is a monitor!
⌨️ Hardware Example 2 — The Keyboard
  • ⌨️Has buttons (keys) you press with your fingers
  • ⌨️You can pick it up and hold it
  • ⌨️Made of plastic with letters printed on top
  • ⌨️Connects to the computer with a wire or wirelessly
HARDWARE ✓ — You CAN touch it!
🎯 Press the letter A on your keyboard right now!
🖱️ Hardware Example 3 — The Mouse
  • 🖱️A small device you hold in your hand
  • 🖱️You move it around on your desk
  • 🖱️Has buttons you click with your fingers
  • 🖱️Made of plastic — it is a real, solid thing
HARDWARE ✓ — You CAN touch it!
🎯 Hold your mouse and give it a wiggle!
🖥️ Hardware Example 4 — The System Unit (CPU Box)
  • 🧠The "brain box" of the computer
  • 🔌All the wires and cables plug into it
  • ⚡Inside: the processor, memory, and storage
  • 🏗️A big metal and plastic box — you can touch it!
HARDWARE ✓ — You CAN touch it!
🤔 Some computers hide this inside a laptop!
🎧 Hardware Example 5 — Headphones
  • 🎧Plays sounds and music into your ears
  • 🎧A real device that you can wear
  • 🎧You can touch them and plug them in
  • 🎧Made of plastic, foam, and small speakers
HARDWARE ✓ — You CAN touch it!
🤔 Have you ever used headphones to listen to music?

💿 What is SOFTWARE?
SOFTWARE
💿
Software = The programs and apps that run INSIDE the computer
  • 🚫You CANNOT touch it — it lives inside the computer
  • 💻It is the programs, apps, and games you use
  • 👀You can SEE it on the screen, but not hold it
  • 🧠The word "SOFT" is inside "Software" — not solid!
💡 Think of your brain! Your thoughts and ideas are like SOFTWARE — you cannot touch them, but they tell your body what to do.
🎨 Software Example 1 — Paint
  • 🎨A drawing program on your computer
  • 🖌️You use it to draw, color, and make art
  • 🚫You can NOT pick it up from the screen
  • 💻It lives INSIDE the computer
SOFTWARE ✓ — You CANNOT touch it!
🎨 Have you ever drawn something on a computer?
🧮 Software Example 2 — Calculator
  • 🧮A program that helps you do math
  • ➕It can add, subtract, multiply, and divide
  • 🚫You see buttons on screen, but cannot touch them for real
  • 💻It is a program inside the computer, not a real thing
SOFTWARE ✓ — You CANNOT touch it!
🤔 What is 5 + 3? Try it in your head!
🌐 Software Example 3 — Web Browser
  • 🌐Opens websites like YouTube and Google
  • 🔍You use it to search and learn new things
  • 🚫The browser is NOT a real object — just a program
  • 💻Examples: Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge
SOFTWARE ✓ — You CANNOT touch it!
🌍 You use a browser every time you go on the internet!
🎮 Software Example 4 — Games
  • 🎮Fun programs you play on a computer or tablet
  • ⭐You see characters and levels on the screen
  • 🚫You CANNOT reach into the screen and grab them
  • 💻Games live INSIDE the computer or device
SOFTWARE ✓ — You CANNOT touch it!
🎮 What is your favorite game? It is Software!
🐱 Software Example 5 — Scratch
  • 🐱A coding program made for kids like you!
  • 🧩You drag colorful blocks to make programs
  • 🎭You can make stories, games, and animations
  • 🚫Scratch is a program — you cannot hold it in your hand
SOFTWARE ✓ — You CANNOT touch it!
🐱 We will use Scratch later in this course!

The Simple Rule 💡
🔧
HARDWARE
You CAN touch it!
Monitor · Keyboard · Mouse · CPU · Printer
⟷
💿
SOFTWARE
You CANNOT touch it!
Paint · Calculator · Browser · Games · Scratch
If you can TOUCH it → Hardware  🔧  If you CANNOT touch it → Software  💿
Real-Life Example: Toy Car 🚗
A computer needs BOTH Hardware AND Software to work!
🚗
The CAR itself = Hardware
You can touch it, hold it, pick it up!
📋
The INSTRUCTIONS inside = Software
They tell the car to go forward, turn, and stop!
🌟 Without Hardware, there is nothing to use.
Without Software, it does not know what to do!
A computer needs BOTH! 🤝

🌟 What We Learned Today!
🔧
Hardware = The physical parts of a computer you CAN touch (Monitor, Keyboard, Mouse, CPU, Printer)
💿
Software = The programs and apps that live INSIDE the computer — you CANNOT touch them (Paint, Calculator, Browser, Games, Scratch)
💡
The Simple Rule: Touch it? → Hardware! Can't touch it? → Software!
🤝
A computer needs BOTH Hardware AND Software to work — like a toy car needs its body AND its instructions!
🗣️
Programming Languages = How we talk to computers to create Software (like Python or Scratch)!
